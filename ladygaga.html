Lady Gaga 

Marvel Stadium, Melbourne - Sat 23 Aug 2014

The Lady Gaga concert in Melbourne 2014 was an absolute spectacle. From the moment we Stepped onto the floor, the anticipation was electric. The venue pulsed with excitement as fans eagerly awaited the arrival of the icon of gay pop.

Her presence was magnetic, commanding attention with every move she made. From her crazy costumes. To her dynamic stage presence, she captivated us from the very first moment.

Throughout the performance, Gaga showcased her insane vocal range and versatility. She effortlessly transitioned from high-energy dance numbers to soul-stirring ballads. Each song was a whole journey, and I loved every second of it.

Even though it was so long ago, I can still vividly remember it as it is such a core part of my memory. Being up on my mum’s shoulders to see the stage was such a bonding experience between us. I would never take that night back for the world. The Lady Gaga concert was more than just a performance. It was an immersive experience that touched hearts and lifted spirits, reminding me of the power of music and art.
